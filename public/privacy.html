Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 26, 2025

Konsulence ("we", "us", "our") operates the DevPos to QuickBooks Online Synchronization Application (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

Important: By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when using our Service:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, company name
  • DevPos Credentials: Tenant ID, username, password (encrypted)
  • QuickBooks Authorization: OAuth tokens for QuickBooks Online access
  • Configuration Data: Sync settings, VAT mappings, date ranges

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our Service, we automatically collect certain information:

  • Usage Data: Sync job history, timestamps, success/failure status
  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, access times, error logs
  • Performance Data: API response times, sync durations

1.3 Data from Third-Party Services

We receive data from integrated services:

  • DevPos: Invoice data, customer/vendor information, tax details
  • QuickBooks Online: Company information, customer lists, transaction status

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

Purpose Information Used
Provide and maintain the Service All collected information
Authenticate API connections DevPos credentials, QuickBooks OAuth tokens
Synchronize data between systems Invoice data, customer information, tax details
Monitor and improve Service performance Usage data, log data, performance metrics
Troubleshoot and provide customer support Error logs, sync history, configuration data
Detect and prevent technical issues Log data, error reports
Comply with legal obligations As required by law

3. Data Security

Security Commitment: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

3.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: All sensitive data (passwords, tokens) is encrypted using AES-256 encryption
  • HTTPS: All data transmission uses SSL/TLS encryption
  • OAuth 2.0: QuickBooks access uses secure OAuth 2.0 protocol
  • Access Controls: Database access is restricted and authenticated
  • Token Expiration: OAuth tokens automatically expire and require refresh
  • Secure Storage: Credentials stored in encrypted format in secure databases

3.2 Data Transmission

  • DevPos API: HTTPS with Bearer token authentication
  • QuickBooks API: HTTPS with OAuth 2.0 authorization
  • Application: Hosted on secure server with SSL certificate
User Responsibility: While we implement strong security measures, you are responsible for:
  • Keeping your account credentials confidential
  • Using strong, unique passwords
  • Logging out after using the Service
  • Reporting any security concerns immediately

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Third-Party Services

We share data only with services necessary for operation:

  • DevPos: We send your credentials to authenticate and retrieve invoice data
  • QuickBooks Online (Intuit): We send invoice data to create transactions in your QuickBooks company
  • Hosting Provider: Our hosting provider (server infrastructure) has access to stored data

4.2 We Do NOT Share Data With:

  • Advertisers or marketing companies
  • Data brokers or analytics companies
  • Social media platforms
  • Any other third parties not listed above

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:

  • Valid legal process (court orders, subpoenas)
  • Government investigations
  • Protection of our rights or property
  • Emergency situations involving safety

5. Data Retention

5.1 Active Account Data

While your account is active, we retain:

  • Account and credential information
  • Sync job history (last 90 days by default)
  • Configuration and mapping data
  • OAuth tokens (until expiration or revocation)

5.2 Data After Account Closure

  • Immediate Deletion: OAuth tokens and API credentials
  • 30-Day Retention: Account information (for recovery purposes)
  • 90-Day Retention: Sync logs and history (for audit purposes)
  • Permanent Deletion: After retention periods expire
